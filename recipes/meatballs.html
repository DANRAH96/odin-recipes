Meatballs

Meatballs with spaghetti and a fresh roasted tomato sauce

Enjoy this delicious recipe that can be made in about 45 minutes, most of the ingredients can be found at home

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Spaghetti
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 200 degree
  2. Put the tomatoes, onion and garlic in a ovenproof container and place in the oven for 30 minutes
  3. Mix the beef with the spices
  4. Make around 20 small meatballs
  5. Fry the meatballs
  6. Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the package
  7. Mix everything together and enjoy!
