Spaghetti carbonara

Classic italian carbonara

Enjoy this delicious recipe that can be made in about 30 minutes that will bring you to Italy, most of the ingredients can be found at home

Ingredients

  • Pancetta
  • Spaghetti
  • Egg yolk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Fry the pancetta
  2. Boil the pasta
  3. Grate the parmesan and mix with egg yolk
  4. Add the pasta to the fried pancetta
  5. Mix a bit of pasta water with the cheese mixture
  6. Mix everything in the pan and add salt and pepper, enjoy!
