Lasagna

Amazing classic italian lasagna

Enjoy this delicious recipe that can be made in about 90 minutes that will bring you to Italy, most of the ingredients can be found at home

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Pasta sheets
  • Parsley
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 200 degree
  2. Mix the tomatoes, onion and garlic in a ovenproof container and place in the oven for 30 minutes
  3. Fry the beef with the spices
  4. Layer the tomato sauce with pasta sheets and beef
  5. Top the lasagna with the mozzarella
  6. Cook in the oven until golden (about 45 minutes) and enjoy!
